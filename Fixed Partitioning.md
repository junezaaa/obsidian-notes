#mmtech
- Main mem is divided into a number of static partitions.
- A proc may be loaded into a partition of **>= size**.

**Strength:**
	- Simple to implement
	- Little sys overhead.
**Weakness:**
	- [[Internal vs External Fragmentation|Internal fragmentation.]]
	- Maximum number of active proc is fixed
